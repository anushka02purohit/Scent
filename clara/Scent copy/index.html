How does it work?

Assurance

With SCENT, you can streamline the process of investing in property, thanks to the integration of Smart Contracts. This makes the process easier and transparent.

Accessibility

All stakeholders within a transaction will have a easy access to the same set of information. Any change will be recorded and have to be approved by all stakeholders, making the smart contract an easier method to record changes and updates.

Affordability

With SCENT, people can finally benefit from appreciation rates of houses, allowing those without homes to have a home, through quick and easy solutions.

Automation

With SCENT, one does not have to periodically monitor their expenses. Through the smart contract, funds are automatically transferred, whether this be in monthly installments or a one time payment to purchase the property.

Relevance

User Friendliness

Despite the sophisticated technology behind SCENT, we have created a simple user interface that is easy to follow, with the complicated parts abstracted away, thus giving users access to powerful technology at their fingertips.

What is SCENT?

SCENT is an amalgamation of Smart Contracts run through a blockchain. SCENT helps to facilitate and digitalize the process of buying and investing in property among multiple title ownership.

?

What problem does SCENT solve?

SCENT helps combat the problem of the lack of affordable housing. Many properties are simply too expensive for a single household income to be able to afford. With the help of SCENT's infrastructure, one can join forces with others to invest in shares of property in order to ease the process of buying property.

Does I need to understand cryptocurrency/blockchain to use SCENT?

No. SCENT integrates a simpler UI that works better for the consumers instead of advertising blockchain. All the complexities of the backend blockchain are abstracted away and handled by the software.
